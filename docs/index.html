About

I graduated from Oxford University with First Class Honours in English in 2017. For the next few years, I worked as editor of the Oxford University Press education website Oxford Owl.

In 2020, I moved to New York to pursue a master's degree in Data Journalism at Columbia University. I am now a data reporter with Mission Local in San Francisco. In 2022, I won the Northern California Society of Professional Journalists' data visualization award.

I have a particular interest in news graphics and building fun data tools. In my spare time, I love hiking, drawing, and writing web bios.
